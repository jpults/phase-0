1. What is a wireframe?

It is a blueprint of the design, attempting to fit in and visualize all the elements required for the site. It is generally very simple so as not to distract from whats important.
2. What are the benefits of wireframing?

You can get a good idea for yourself and others of where your design is heading.  You can see potential problems in the design and anticipate challenges that will need to be overcome.  
3. Did you enjoy wireframing your site?

Sure. It was fun to play around creatively without having to code the html straight away.
